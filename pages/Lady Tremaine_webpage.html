  • Lady Tremaine is Cinderella's step mother, and she is also the main protagonist of the Disney classic, Cinderella.
  • Before getting married to Cinderella's father, Lady Tremaine was once married to Sir Francis Tremaine and bore two daughters, Anastasia and Drizella.
  • Lady Tremaine treats Cinderella lesser than that of a slave. She makes Cinderella do all the household chores with no regards for Cinderella's well-being.
